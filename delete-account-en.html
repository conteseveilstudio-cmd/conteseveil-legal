Use this page to request deletion of your parent account and related data, as required by Google Play and privacy laws (account managed by an adult).

1. How to request deletion

Recommended — email:

Send a message to conteseveil.studio@gmail.com including:

  • Subject: Delete ContesÉveil account
  • The exact email of the account to delete
  • Confirmation that you are the account holder (parent / guardian)

Send deletion request by email

2. From the app

You may also contact us from Profile → Parental space / Support and ask for account deletion.

3. Processing time

  • We process requests within a reasonable time, typically within 30 days.
  • We may ask you to confirm account ownership (account email).

4. What is deleted

  • Parent account (email, profile, preferences)
  • Story metadata and related server content
  • Credits / promo history linked to the account (subject to minimal legal retention)

5. What may be kept briefly

  • Billing / payment records if required by law
  • Copies still on the device (My Magic Book) — remove by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

6. After deletion

You will no longer be able to sign in with that email. A new account is required to use the app again.
