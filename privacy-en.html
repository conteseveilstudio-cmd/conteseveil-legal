1. Who we are

ContesÉveil Studio (“we”) operates the mobile app ContesÉveil Magique (English name: Magic Spark Tales), a personalized story service with audio, illustrations and coloring pages for children about ages 3–6, under parental control.

Privacy & support: conteseveil.studio@gmail.com

2. Audience & parental account

  • The account is created and managed by an adult (usually 18+).
  • The child is not the contracting user.
  • At sign-up, the parent confirms adult status and accepts the Terms, this Policy, and child safety rules.

3. Data we collect

3.1 Parent account

  • Email address
  • Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Language / story dialect preferences
  • Sign-up consents
  • Parental PIN (if enabled), stored securely

3.2 Child information provided by the parent

  • First name (optional, for personalization)
  • Approximate age / narrative role (boy / girl / neutral) if provided

Used only to personalize educational content—not for a public child profile or child-targeted ads.

3.3 Generated content & usage

  • Wizard choices (theme, characters, personalities, season, duration, keywords, voice…)
  • Generated stories, titles, metadata
  • Audio narration files
  • Story images and coloring pages
  • On-device album (“My Magic Book”)
  • Content reports from parents
  • Light quiz / story interactions if used

3.4 Payments

  • Purchase history, credits, promo codes
  • Processed by providers (e.g. Stripe and/or Google Play Billing / Apple)
  • We do not store full card numbers on our servers when using certified processors

3.5 Technical data

  • Session / auth tokens
  • Technical logs needed for reliability and security

3.6 What we do not require (current version)

  • No camera access for the core experience
  • No mandatory device photo gallery access to create a story
  • No behavioral advertising targeted at children

4. Purposes

  • Provide and improve the service (stories, audio, images, coloring, album)
  • Security, abuse prevention, “Child Protected” content rules
  • Payments, support, legal compliance

5. Legal bases

Depending on your region: contract performance, consent (including parental), legitimate interests (security), and legal obligations. Reference frameworks include GDPR principles (where applicable), PIPEDA (Canada), Quebec Law 25, and COPPA-oriented parental control principles (US).

6. Artificial intelligence

We use third-party AI providers to generate:

  • Story text
  • Voice narration (e.g. OpenAI TTS / ElevenLabs-class services)
  • Images and coloring pages

Prompts include child-safety rules. No system is perfect—parents can report unsuitable content. Content is for family educational entertainment under parental supervision.

7. Child protection

  • Parental account and purchases
  • “Child Protected” generation rules
  • No child-targeted behavioral ads
  • In-app content reporting in parental space

8. Sharing & processors

We do not sell personal data. We share only with:

  • Hosting / databases (e.g. API host, MongoDB Atlas)
  • Media storage if used
  • AI providers (text, voice, image)
  • Payment processors (Stripe, Play, Apple as applicable)
  • Authorities when required by law or to protect safety and rights

9. Storage & retention

  • Device: stories/colorings may be saved in the on-device album
  • Server: account metadata and content needed for delivery/sync
  • Heavy media retention: purge policies may apply after a defined period, while prioritizing on-device keeps when possible
  • Account: kept while active; deletion/anonymization within a reasonable time after closure request, subject to legal retention (e.g. billing)

10. Security

HTTPS, hashed passwords, access controls, and content filters. No security is absolute.

11. Your rights

Depending on law: access, correction, deletion, restriction/objection, portability, and withdrawal of consent. Email conteseveil.studio@gmail.com with subject “Privacy / rights”.

12. International transfers

Processors may handle data outside your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards.

13. Tracking & advertising

  • Not built around classic web cookies
  • No behavioral ads targeting children
  • Optional anonymous analytics may be added later; this Policy would be updated

14. Changes

We may update this Policy. The date at the top will change. Material changes may be communicated in-app or by email. The published version prevails.
